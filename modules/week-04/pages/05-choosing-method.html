Choosing a method

How to choose a method

  1. Explore the distribution (histogram / summary stats).
  2. Start with Natural breaks to see clusters.
  3. Try Quantile and Equal interval for comparison.
  4. Use 5–7 classes by default.
  5. Pick the option you can justify in a caption.

Natural breaks intuition

Jenks chooses breaks to minimize within-class variance. Break widths vary, but patterns can be clearer for skewed data.
