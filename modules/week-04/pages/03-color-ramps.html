Color ramps

Color should match the data

  • Categorical: different hues (distinct colors)
  • Quantitative: light→dark (value/saturation of the same hue)
  • Diverging: two directions away from a meaningful midpoint (e.g., change from 0)

Do

  • Use change in value/saturation to indicate magnitude
  • Keep classes to ~5–7 for readability
  • Ensure enough contrast between classes

Don’t

  • Avoid rainbow schemes for quantities
  • Avoid too many classes
  • Avoid “muddy” ramps where classes look identical
