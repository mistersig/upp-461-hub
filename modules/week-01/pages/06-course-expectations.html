Course + expectations

What to expect in UPP 461

Before class

  • Readings and textbook practice exercises (technical skills)

During class

  • Lecture + discussion on GIS concepts
  • Demos and guided practice
  • Collaboration and troubleshooting

Assignments (graded)

  • Weekly practice exercises (textbook)
  • Weekly lab assignments starting Week 4
  • Final project with milestones starting Week 10

Expectations

  • Be responsible for your learning.
  • Know the syllabus. Use it first for policies and procedures.
  • Manage your data. Your file organization affects your success.
  • Communicate early. Don’t wait for issues to pile up.
  • Meet deadlines. Plan around Help Desk / TA availability.

How to succeed

  • Use resources: ArcGIS Help menu, Help Desk, TA sessions
  • Start assignments early and pace yourself
  • Document your work (project log + screenshots of issues)
  • Embrace the struggle. Troubleshooting is part of GIS.

Technical difficulties

They will happen. Your job is to capture evidence and seek help effectively.

  1. Take a screenshot and note exact error messages.
  2. Record what you tried (and what changed).
  3. Ask for help if you can’t solve it.
Class norm: We’ll be patient and flexible, while also holding high standards for our work.
