Geography & why it matters

Geography

Geography is the study of Earth’s landscapes, environments, places, and the spatial relationships between them.

Tobler’s Law (rule of thumb)

“Everything is related to everything else, but near things are more related than distant things.”

Urban geography

Studies cities and urban areas: spatial structure, development patterns, social dynamics, and interactions with surrounding environments.

Urban planning

A multidisciplinary field focused on the design, development, and management of urban areas to create functional, sustainable, and livable communities.

Bridge to GIS: GIS helps planners describe patterns (where?) and test processes (why/how?).
