Remember: A datum defines the Earth model and how coordinates “sit” on it. A projection is how we flatten the curved Earth onto a map.

Learning goals

  • Explain what a Coordinate Reference System (CRS) is.
  • Distinguish Geographic Coordinate System (GCS) vs Projected Coordinate System (PCS).
  • Describe what a datum does and why changing it can shift data.
  • Choose a projection based on the task (area, distance, direction, shape).
