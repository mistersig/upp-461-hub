Case study + your final project workflow

Case: identify priority neighborhoods for a city-owned grocery store in Chicago under a food equity initiative.

Case study goal

Goal: Use spatial data and stakeholder input to support evidence-based site selection.

Constraints to clarify

  • Timeline and decision deadline
  • What data is available (and at what geography?)
  • Definition of “need” (metrics + thresholds)
  • Stakeholder priorities (equity, access, cost)

Hybrid lifecycle: what happens in each phase?

Initiation

  • Define problem + success metrics
  • Identify stakeholders
  • Confirm constraints (timeline, data access)

Planning & execution

  • Build a milestone plan
  • Iterate on analysis and maps (demos)
  • Document data and decisions in a project log

Monitoring & control

  • Weekly check-ins
  • Kanban board (To Do / In Progress / Waiting / Done)
  • Midpoint stakeholder validation

Closure

  • Deliver final map/report + methods
  • Handoff documentation
  • Reflect: what would you improve?
