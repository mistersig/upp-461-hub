Map design principles

Figure–ground effect

Use visual differences (color, size, line weight, pattern, drawing order) so the most important features become the figure and everything else becomes ground.

Try it: figure–ground builder

Adjust settings to make the “message layer” pop without making the map noisy.

Goal: Figure is obvious in 1–2 seconds. Ground supports orientation.

Contrast tips

  • Value (light vs dark): higher contrast makes features easier to separate.
  • Warm vs cool: warm colors often “advance,” cool colors often “recede.”
  • Hierarchy: bright/bold for key features; drab/thin for context.
