Graphicacy

Graphicacy

Graphicacy is the ability to understand and communicate through graphics (maps, charts, diagrams).

  • Maps are persuasive: design choices affect what people believe.
  • Planning and policy decisions often depend on how spatial data is presented.

Map design is abstraction

Maps can’t show everything. Making a map means selecting what to include and using design to communicate it.

Abstraction = transforming geographic information into a representation that supports a purpose.

Check yourself

When you change color, labels, or drawing order, you are changing what the reader notices first.

Prompt: What is the “one thing” your map should say?
