Summary

Week 3 checklist

  • What is the map’s purpose and audience?
  • Does the figure pop (message is obvious)?
  • Is there enough contrast for labels and boundaries?
  • Does the color scheme match the data type and remain accessible?
  • Are layout elements sized by importance (map + title dominate)?
  • Credits and sources included?

Common mistakes

  • Too many bright layers competing for attention.
  • Legend includes everything (instead of what needs interpretation).
  • Red–green diverging ramps and low-contrast labels.
  • Title is generic (“Map of…”), missing where/when.
